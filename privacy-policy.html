Privacy Policy

1. Information Collected

SignalDev Labs may collect contact details and project information submitted through the consultation form, including name, email, company, phone number, and technical context related to the inquiry.

2. Use of Information

Information is used to respond to consultation requests, understand project requirements, schedule discussions, and provide engineering services.

3. Confidential Technical Details

Please do not submit export-controlled, proprietary, or confidential design data through the public form unless an appropriate agreement is in place. Sensitive files should be exchanged through an agreed secure channel.

4. Data Sharing

SignalDev Labs does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only with service providers needed to operate the website or deliver requested services.

5. SMS Communications

If you provide a phone number and check the SMS consent box on the contact form, SignalDev Labs may send SMS messages related to your consultation request, scheduling, confirmations, or service updates. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help. SMS consent is not a condition of purchasing services.

Mobile opt-in information and SMS consent will not be shared with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, please contact SignalDev Labs.
